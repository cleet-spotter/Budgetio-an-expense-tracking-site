What is Budgetio?

Budgetio is a full-featured investment manager. It tracks and categorizes all the expenses you make. Budgetio is great for making expense reports on the go indicating your monthly cash flow and breaking down your monthly expenses. It also has an article section where one can read about wealth management, personal finance and more. The free financial dashboard provides budgeting and a cash flow analyzer.
